Weekly Program

Our usual weekly programs are held on Sundays from 2:00 PM - 5:30 PM

  • 2:00 - 3:15 : Sai Spiritual Education for Children (Sep to June)
  • 3:30 - 3:35 : Multi-faith Prayers
  • 3:35 - 4:25 : Bhajans (Devotional Singing)
  • 4:25 - 4:35 : Arati, Closing Prayers, Welcome and Announcements
  • 4:40 - 5:30 : Study circles
  • 5:30 to 5:40 : Wrap up/clean-up

Even though this is the typical program, it can vary sometimes. So, please feel free to contact us at cherryhillsaicenter@gmail.com for the current events.
