Education

        THE OBJECTIVES OF SSE AS DEFINED BY BABA ARE:
  • To raise a generation of boys and girls with a clean and clear conscience.
  • To guide the children in the spiritual path through simple and moral living.
  • To install in the young minds the importance of Duty, Devotion and Discipline.
  • To help them to Love all and Serve all.
  • To help them to grow into worthy citizens, to serve the society, identifying the Divinity present in all.
  • In short, to raise a generation of torch bearers of Sai ideals.
  • These children are the future leaders of the Organization who can make a difference in the world.
