Mindful Parenting

Mindful Parenting Timings and Details:

Swami Says, "The home and the family is the basic social institution everywhere in the world. When the home improves, the whole world will be better. "
This group focuses on providing the parents with the necessary tools & skills to integrate the human values of love, peace, truth, right conduct & non violence into their basic character and the character of their children, which would then enable them to face effectively the present day parenting challenges and to enable insights to unlearn, unhealthy parenting practices.The group discusses many different topics such as discipline, patience, and many hot parenting topics.
