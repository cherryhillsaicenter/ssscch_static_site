"I have come to light the lamp of Love in your hearts, to see that it shines day by day with added luster. I have not come on behalf of any exclusive religion. I have not come on a mission of publicity for a sect or creed or cause, nor have I come to collect followers for a doctrine. I have no plan to attract disciples or devotees into my fold or any fold. I have come to tell you of this unitary faith, this spiritual principle, this path of Love, this virtue of Love, this duty of Love, this obligation of Love." - Sathya Sai Baba on 4 July 1968.

Our Center is involved in Devotional, Educational and Service programs. Participation in the Center's activities is purely voluntary and is open to all. There are no fees of any kind to join or participate in the activities. Sathya Sai Baba's life and teachings have inspired millions around the globe and we welcome you to explore this spiritual journey with us.

The purpose of our center is to create a spiritual space to study and practice the teachings of Sathya sai baba. It is a non-denominational, non-religious, purely spiritual and service-oriented community. The Center welcomes all spiritual seekers irrespective of their background and religious affiliation.

Samastha Lokha Sukino Bhavanthu!
May all the beings in all the worlds be happy !
