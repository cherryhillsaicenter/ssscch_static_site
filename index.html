SSE

"The end of education is character. Education without character is useless." -- Sathya Sai Baba, Prashanthi Nilayam, November 20, 2001.

"Bal Vikas (Sai Spiritual Education) is the primary basis of the great movement to restore righteousness (dharma) in the world. The ideal of the program is to raise a generation of boys and girls who have a clean and a clear conscience."

Cherry Hill SSE Timings and Details:

(Sai Spiritual Education) meets every Sunday from 2:00 - 3:15 pm!

  • Pre SSE (4 to 6 Yrs Old)
  • Group 1 (1st, 2nd, 3rd Graders)
  • Group 2 (4th, 5th , 6th Graders)
  • Group 3 (7th, 8th, 9th Graders)
  • Group 4 (10th, 11th , 12th Graders)
  • Young Adults (18 to 40 yrs old)
All our teachers are certified in teaching SSE. If you need more information please contact cherryhillsaicenter@gmail.com
