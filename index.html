About Us

A Community Rooted in
Love & Service

The Cherry Hill Sai Center is dedicated to promoting spiritual growth and community service. We follow the teachings of Sri Sathya Sai Baba and aim to create a loving and compassionate environment.

Our center is part of the Sri Sathya Sai International Organization and serves devotees and seekers of all backgrounds in the Cherry Hill, New Jersey area.

"To foster a loving community through spiritual education and selfless service."

— Our Mission

Bal Vikas

Sai Spiritual Education (SSE)

Character-based education for children and young adults, grounded in Sai Baba's five human values.

"The end of education is character. Education without character is useless."

— Sathya Sai Baba, Prashanthi Nilayam, November 20, 2001

"Bal Vikas (Sai Spiritual Education) is the primary basis of the great movement to restore righteousness (dharma) in the world. The ideal of the program is to raise a generation of boys and girls who have a clean and a clear conscience."

— Sri Sathya Sai Baba
✅

Young Adults

Sri Sathya Sai International
Young Adults Program

The Sri Sathya Sai Young Adults Programme (SSSYAP) seeks to deliver Swami's powerful message to the world through Young Adults worldwide.

The Programme encourages men and women, between the ages of 18 and 40, to lead purposeful lives in service of their families, communities, and countries by learning and practicing the spiritual principles revealed by the life, teachings, and work of Sri Sathya Sai Baba.

Primary importance is given to holistic character development through both personal transformation and selfless service.

"Young men and women, the world's good fate, world's progress is dependent on the transformation of the youth, today. Only when the youth transform themselves with good character, will the world be able to experience ideal virtues."

— Sri Sathya Sai Baba, World Youth Conference, 1997
